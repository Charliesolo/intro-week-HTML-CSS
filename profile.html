Sherlock Holmes' Profile

Sherlock Holmes

Publishing history

Sherlock Holmes is a fictional detective, perhaps the most famous detective in all of fiction. He was created by Arthur Conan Doyle.

He first appeared in 'A Study in Scarlet' in 1887, a short story by Arthur Conan Doyle. Doyle published four novels and 56 short stories featuring Holmes.

Short Stories

An example of some Sherlock Holmes short stories include:

    A Scandal in Bohemia
    A Case of Identity
    The Five Orange Pips

Novels

The four novels of the canon are:

    A study in Scarlet (1887)
    The Sign of the Four (1890)
    The Hound of the Baskervilles (1901-1902)
    The Valley of Fear (1914 - 1915)
